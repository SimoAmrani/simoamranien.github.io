Dedicated primary school teacher with 7 years of experience, I stand out for my passion for teaching and my exceptional commitment to student success. Possessing proven patience and a remarkable ability to interact with children, I have built my career on inspiring students and continually improving learning outcomes. I creatively integrate Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) into my classroom to enhance the learning experience. Recognized as a STEM educator, I actively engage in coaching teams for robotics competitions, showcasing my versatility. Ready to leverage my educational expertise and skills to make a meaningful contribution.
